About Us

A center of excellence for value-based education

Our Purpose

Vision and Mission

Our Vision

To create disciplined, knowledgeable, and responsible citizens rooted in Indian culture who can lead the nation to a prosperous future.

Our Mission

To provide quality education while nurturing character, leadership abilities, and national values among students.

Our Foundations

Core Values

Discipline

The cornerstone of success and character development in all spheres of life.

Integrity

Maintaining high moral standards, absolute honesty, and transparency in thought and action.

Service

Fostering a spirit of social responsibility, helpfulness, and community support.

Patriotism

Nurturing a deep love for the nation, respect for national symbols, and civic pride.

Respect

Promoting mutual respect for teachers, parents, peers, and diverse viewpoints.

Excellence

Striving continuously to achieve the highest standards in academics, arts, and physical activities.

Our Heritage

Vidya Bharati Tradition

Govindanand Adarsh Vidya Mandir is affiliated with the Vidya Bharati Akhil Bharatiya Shiksha Sansthan. Our core value is rooted in providing value-based, character-focused education. We cultivate national pride, cultural awareness, and ethical development alongside standard school curricula.

To ensure the physical, mental, intellectual, and spiritual growth of our students, we organize regular yoga assemblies, morning prayers, and cultural programs as part of our daily school life.
