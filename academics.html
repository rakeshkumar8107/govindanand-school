Academic Structure

Primary to Senior Secondary

Govindanand Adarsh Vidya Mandir is affiliated with the Board of Secondary Education Rajasthan (BSER). We deliver quality education embedded with Indian cultural values from primary levels to senior secondary board examinations. After completing class 10, students have access to our excellent Arts Stream.

Arts Stream Only

*Please note: The school offers exclusively the Arts Stream at the senior secondary level (Class 11 & 12).
