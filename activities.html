School Activities

Cultural development, sportsmanship, and value-based learning

Co-Curricular Events

Cultural, Sports & Environmental Activities

Annual Event

Annual Function

The annual function is celebrated with great joy and enthusiasm. It features diverse cultural performances, school dramas, patriotic songs, and the annual student prize distribution ceremony.

Artistic Development

Cultural Programs

Aligned with Vidya Bharati values, students get multiple platforms to build their talents in classical music, group folk dances, debate events, and student assemblies.

Daily Routine

Prayer Assembly

Every school day begins with our traditional prayer assembly. Students gather in rows to recite the Saraswati Vandana and Gayatri Mantra, developing positive focus and moral values.

Physical Fitness

Sports Day

Features annual field contests in games like Kabaddi, Kho-Kho, athletics, chess, and races, encouraging sportsmanship and team collaboration.

Discipline & Parade

Student Parade

During Independence Day and Republic Day celebrations, our marching band leads a highly disciplined student parade, showing our dedication to order and patriotism.

Nature Protection

Environmental Protection Initiatives

Students actively participate in tree plantation drives, school cleaning programs, and plastic-free awareness campaigns to build respect and duty for nature.
