Infrastructure

Well-equipped labs and a highly supportive educational environment

Our Environment

School Campus & Classrooms

School Campus

The school campus is built in the serene, clean, and peaceful valleys of Abu Road. We prioritize safety, proper ventilation, and absolute cleanliness throughout the classrooms and corridors.

Spacious Classrooms

Our classrooms are wide, spacious, and well-lit with abundant natural light. The desk layouts are structured to create a highly focused and engaging atmosphere for studies.

Applied Learning

Laboratories & Halls

Computer Laboratory

Our computer lab features multiple systems with technical software applications. Students begin technical training and digital learning from class 6 onward under expert guidance.

Atal Tinkering Lab

Sponsored by NITI Aayog (Govt. of India), the ATL provides students with resources to learn coding, electronics, robotics, 3D printing, and to construct innovative science models.

Additional Facilities

Assembly Hall, Sports Ground & Library

Prayer Hall

A large, serene prayer hall is dedicated to morning assemblies, mantras recitation, moral education classes, and collective meditation programs.

Sports Ground

Our spacious outdoor sports field is used for daily physical drills, parade practice, and games like Kabaddi, Kho-Kho, and general athletics.

School Library

Houses an extensive collection of books on literature, science, history, morals, and academic journals to support self-study and learning.

Bus Service

Safe and reliable transportation is available for students from all major routes and neighborhoods across Abu Road and nearby areas.
